E-commerce tools for ordering regalia.

Introduction

Graduation ceremonies are milestone moments that require proper academic regalia. However, the traditional ordering process for caps, gowns, and accessories was often complicated and time-consuming for both students and institutions.

The redesigned e-commerce platform streamlined the ordering experience, making it easier for graduates to find the right regalia while ensuring institutions could manage bulk orders efficiently.
